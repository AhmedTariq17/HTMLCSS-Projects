Must Dine Restaurants

Let's EAT!

If you love food, Dubai is the place to go. You are offered an abundance of cuisine made with the highest quality ingredients, including fine dining and fast food places which produce much higher quality products.

Al-Baik

Al-Baik is an extremely popular fast-food restaurant in Dubai, known for its fried chicken. It is located inside the Dubai Mall, and sometimes people have to wait in line for a couple of hours before getting their food. People in the Gulf region swear that it is the best fried chicken in the world. The restaurant originated in Saudi Arabia and was only available there until the first location was opened in Dubai. For those who have lived or visited Saudi Arabia, everyone always recommends trying Al-Baik as it is also very nostalgic for many expats who may have left Saudi Arabia. The restaurant being in Dubai, which is a travel hub for the world, gives many people the opportunity to go out on a layover and try one of the most popular fried chicken places in the world.

CZN Burak

CZN Burak is a Turkish fine dining restaurant whose owner you most likely have seen while scrolling on social media. The owner and chef at the restaurant, Burak Özdemir, is a famous media personality, as he has posted videos of himself creating ginormous foods with a big smile on his face, and these videos have amassed millions of views online. The popularity of his videos matches that of his restaurant, and for good reason, as delicious Turkish meats, desserts, and cocktails are served and enjoyed by everyone who dines there.

Ristorante L’Olivo

Ristorante L’Olivo at Al Mahara is a seafood Italian restaurant located in the Palm Jumeirah at the famous Burj Al Arab hotel. It is a Michelin-starred restaurant which has recently been introduced to Dubai. However, the reviews for this place have been incredible. People who have dined here have expressed their love for the ambiance and atmosphere of the restaurant and are delighted with the food made by Chef Andrea Migliaccio. One of the big drawing points of this restaurant is the aquarium view that you get when eating, as it is described to emmulate the feeling about being at the waters of Capri, Italy.

Nobu Dubai

Nobu is a well-recognized restaurant name, synonymous with serving the most flavorful food made of exotic and expensive ingredients. With restaurants located around the world, including famous cities such as New York, Los Angeles, and London, Nobu has become a household name. They are most famous for their miso black cod, which is cod marinated in miso for three days, bringing out a wonderful flavor that must be tried. Although this restaurant is not exclusive to Dubai, there are still reasons to visit. Nobu Dubai incorporates Middle Eastern flavoring, offering a unique taste to their food that sets it apart from other Nobu locations, making it a must-visit place when in Dubai.
