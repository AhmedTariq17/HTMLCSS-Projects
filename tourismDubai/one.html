Attractions In The City Of Dubai

The Oasis That is Dubai

Dubai is a city that never ceases to amaze with its endless array of activities and attractions. Known as the city of the future, Dubai is a place where you'll never be bored thanks to the sheer volume of things to see and do. Whether you're interested in exploring the marvels of modern architecture or seeking adventure, Dubai has something for everyone. At every turn, you'll encounter a world record feat, from the world's tallest building to the largest mall. With such impressive sights and experiences on offer, Dubai is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a taste of the extraordinary.

Burj Khalifa

For starters, one site of the city which cannot truly be missed, and is a must-visit place, is the world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa. This freestanding structure is the biggest ever in history to be created by man, standing at an astounding 828 meters tall, which is almost a kilometer in height. The Burj Khalifa can be seen throughout the city; however, being up close with the building is a unique experience in itself, especially at night where the building is projecting with sparkles lighting up the skyline. Although the view from the outside is incredible, the Burj Khalifa offers a lot from the inside as well, having the world's highest observation deck which overlooks the whole horizon, and the entire city of Dubai can be viewed from it. Tours of the building are accessible; yet, if you want a more special experience, you can do activities in the Burj such as gaining access to the luxurious Armani spa to unwind and relax during your trip to the city. After visiting the spa, you may feel a bit hungry, so you can help yourself by visiting the world's highest restaurant called Altitude. Overall, the Burj Khalifa is a must-visit when coming to Dubai, and the experience will be remembered for a lifetime.

Dubai Mall

Being in the most opulent city in the world, you must be wondering if Dubai has any good malls. The answer to that is yes, Dubai is home to the best and biggest mall in the entire world, the Dubai Mall. Nothing about this mall is similar to that of your local mall, as everything in the Dubai Mall screams flamboyance and extravagance. The Dubai Mall contains over 1,200 stores, containing any luxury brand imaginable, and 200 restaurants, which showcase food from around the world, reflecting the diversity of people who live in the city. The mall is also home to formerly the world's biggest aquarium, located right in the middle of the mall, where people can walk through and be up close and surrounded by beautiful marine wildlife. The mall also has attractions for everyone, such as a cinema, a haunted house, an opera, and a trampoline park, just to name a few.

Palm Jumeirah

The leadership of the United Arab Emirates are visionaries, and their creativity is expressed through the wonderful city of Dubai. Their ambition to make Dubai a unique city, separate from the rest of the world, can be best exemplified through the creation of the Palm Jumeirah. This man-made island, shaped like a palm tree, is the largest man-made island in the world and is truly breathtaking when observed from a bird's eye view. This island is home to many magnificent hotels and homes for Dubai residents, but it is also a place riddled with fun activities. One of the world's only 7-star hotels, Atlantis, is located on the Palm, and you can gain access to its world-famous water park. The water park, just like the other places mentioned in this list, is one of the largest waterparks in the world and is a great destination for families, as it is a wonderland for children. Skydiving is an extreme sport and can be done all around the world; however, the skydiving experience on the Palm is unlike any other, as when you are coming down, you have a magical bird's eye view of the Palm alongside the crystal clear waters surrounding Dubai. If you are not interested in such extreme activities, the Palm also has more calming exercises, such as taking a paddleboat in the waters with your friends, overlooking the scenery of what the Palm has to offer.

Burj Al Arab

Last but not least, Dubai is home to the world's first-ever 7-star hotel, the Burj Al Arab. This hotel is the epitome of luxury, as only the finest materials such as Statuario marble, gold leaf, and lighting fixtures made out of Swarovski crystals, were used in the construction of the hotel. Upon walking into the hotel, you are greeted with world-renowned staff giving you the best customer service experience of your life. Although the glitz and glamour of this establishment can possibly be slightly overwhelming, there is history and significance to this hotel as well. The infamous helipad located just above three-quarters of the building has had many cool things happen on it. Red Bull, in specific, have done many stunts such as doing donuts in a Formula One car on the helipad celebrating a grand prix win. Most recently, they had landed a plane as well as taken off from the helipad. All of these things combined make the Burj Al Arab a must-see when visiting Dubai.
